Mongo first, seed when not.

Every section of every page goes through one pick() decision. When a Mongo collection has zero documents — or the cluster is unreachable — the section transparently falls back to the in-repo SEED constant.

The green path is the desired prod state; the muted fallback path is what keeps the site green when Mongo is empty, unreachable, or — as on Atlas right now — at the cluster-wide collection cap.

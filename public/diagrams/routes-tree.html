DIAGRAM · ROUTE TREE

Twenty-six routes, three modes.

Public ISR routes (rebuilt every 5 minutes), dynamic admin routes (re-rendered per request), and the auth surfaces. The route group (v2) shares one client shell across every section.

All (v2) leaves are statically prerendered with 5-minute ISR; bo/* leaves are force-dynamic so they re-render the live Mongo state on every request.

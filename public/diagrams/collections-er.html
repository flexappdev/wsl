Fourteen collections, eight pages.

The WSL site reads from fourteen wsl_* Mongo collections. Twelve are currently seeded; wsl_scroller and wsl_hotels fall back to SEED because the cluster is at its 500-collection cap.

Filled green badges are live on the cluster. Two dashed seed badges denote sections that need extra cluster room (or a schema collapse into wsl_countries.hotels[] and wsl_facts.scroller[]) before they can be promoted to Mongo.

DIAGRAM · DATA HIERARCHY

Three sources, one entry, predictable shape.

Each WikivoyageEntry is the merge of up to three data sources: editorial text from Wikivoyage or Wikipedia, a FLUX-generated hero from Runware on S3, and structured metadata (region, code, flag) always sourced from the static SEED.

SEED is the only source that never fails — it ships in the bundle. Wikivoyage and Wikipedia are both file-cached under .cache/wikivoyage/ to make re-runs idempotent and offline-friendly. The FLUX pipeline runs as a separate npm run gen:heroes step and stamps s3_hero=true on the entry when the JPG is up.
